❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I make a booking?

Go to the Bookings page, fill in your details, select a property and confirm. You'll receive a confirmation via email or call 0722 986 754.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes! Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before check-in. Contact us at smarhomesbnbs@gmail.com or call us to process your cancellation.

How do I list my property?

Send us an email at smarhomesbnbs@gmail.com with photos and details of your property. Our team will review and get back to you within 48 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, Visa, Mastercard and bank transfers. For M-Pesa payments, call 0722 986 754 for the paybill details.
