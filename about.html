Our Story

🔥

🌱 How It All Started

SmartHomes BnBs was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate Kenyan travelers who were frustrated by the lack of quality, affordable short-term rentals. We set out to change that — one cozy home at a time.

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is to connect travelers with authentic, comfortable homes across Kenya while empowering local homeowners to earn income from their properties. We believe every traveler deserves a place that feels like home.

🌍 Our Vision

We envision a Kenya where every corner of the country is accessible to travelers through a network of trusted, verified, and beautifully maintained homes. From Turkana to Mombasa — we're building that future.

💡 What Makes Us Smart

The "Smart" in SmartHomes isn't just about technology — it's about smart choices: smart pricing, smart hosts, smart experiences. We use data and community feedback to continuously improve every listing on our platform.

🤝 Community First

We are deeply committed to the communities we operate in. A portion of every booking goes to local community development funds, supporting schools, clean water projects, and small businesses near our properties.
